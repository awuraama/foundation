WHO WE ARE

Missions & Values

Empowering Lives, Building Futures

Our Mission

The mission of SarkAkyere Foundation (SAF) is to give hope and build a better future for others by investing in child well-being, churches, orphanages, prisons and providing help in diverse ways.

SAF believes that humanity deserves the best and children especially deserve a future. Such concept of SAF is the core principles for humanity supports. We are transforming the way support for humanity is delivered, putting trained professionals to work for humanity in crisis anywhere of this world. SAF is focused on championing gender equality and transforming lives.

The SarkAkyere Foundation (SAF) operates the following grant-making programs:

  • Child Well – Being: SAF believes that children have the right to live right, transformed and be educated. SAF focuses on empowering children and ensuring they live to establish their God given talents.
  • Churches: SAF has over the years partnered with churches to help transform lives. This partnership seeks to improve love for humanity and to foster an inclusive conservative movement.
  • Orphanages: SAF continuously seeks to support orphanages to ensure children have a happy life in challenging moments of their lives. SAF focuses on helping more children in crises to recover and returning to positive transformative ways.
  • Prisons: SAF is working with prisons to help positively train inmates to become reformed for our societies. SAF believes that inmates have the right to education, well – being and reformed towards positive ways.
  • Community: SAF is working with all professionals to increase mutual understanding of human life and well – being among diverse populations to foster stronger and inclusive communities in Africa and beyond.

Our Values

SAF is guided by the below values to effectively operate for humanity:

  • Honesty: SAF relies on honesty and ethical concepts in all aspects of delivering supports. SAF remains accountable to all stakeholders and entities being engaged.
  • Excellence: In fulfilling our missions, SAF engages the best standards and practices to ensure humanitarian supports. Decisions are critically reviewed based on available information.
  • Teamwork: SAF values Teamwork to ensure operational efficiency. Creating a working environment that promotes trust, honesty and respect within SAF is as important as earning the trust and respect of our colleagues and peers in the fields in which we work.
  • Diversity, Equity and Inclusion: SAF is committed to diversity, equity and inclusion. SAF focuses on working towards an inclusive society and is committed to the values of diversity, equity, inclusion, and social justice of humanity.

Our Founder(s)

This foundation was seeded by Mr Richard Sarkodie Amo, IEng in 2020 post the COVID-19 season. He started this foundation to support the needy and giving them hope for the future. Mrs Ama Akyere Amo (his Wife) joined him after marriage to support the foundation and to advance it for humanity. The blessed couple are following their God given mission of helping others in diverse ways and are committed to doing so wholeheartedly.

Our History

Right from their childhood, Mr. and Mrs. Amo (Founder and Co-Founder) have had the passion of helping others. They have contributed immensely to building churches, promoting well-being of children, and helping others immensely. The SAF is committed to providing help and support in diverse ways.
